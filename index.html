Privacy Policy

Kairos (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Kairos mobile application. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: July 9th, 2026.

Summary

Kairos is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not require an account. We do not sell your personal information.

Information stored on your device

The app may store locally on your phone or tablet:

  • Display name (optional)
  • App preferences (language, theme, reminder time)
  • Reading progress, streaks, saved verses, and reflection usage
  • Onboarding and in-app guide state

This data stays on your device unless you delete the app or reset app data.

Information processed by third parties

Apple processes in-app purchases and subscriptions when you buy Kairos Premium. Apple’s privacy policy applies to those transactions.

RevenueCat helps us manage subscriptions and premium access. RevenueCat may receive purchase-related identifiers. See their policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Permissions

  • Notifications: optional, for daily reminders you configure.
  • Photo library: only if you choose to save a shared image to your gallery.

What we do not do

  • We do not run advertising based on your data.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not require you to create an account.

Children

Kairos is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention and deletion

Local app data is removed when you uninstall the app. Purchase records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat according to their policies.

Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Contact us and we will help where we can.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new version at this URL and update the “Last updated” date.
