Privacy Policy

Kairos (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Kairos mobile application. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: July 11th, 2026.

Summary

Kairos is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not require an account. We do not sell your personal information. Kairos shows optional rewarded ads (which you choose to watch) served by Google AdMob; we request non-personalized ads only.

Information stored on your device

The app may store locally on your phone or tablet:

  • Display name (optional)
  • App preferences (language, theme, reminder time)
  • Reading progress, streaks, saved verses, and reflection usage
  • Onboarding and in-app guide state

This data stays on your device unless you delete the app or reset app data.

Information processed by third parties

Apple processes in-app purchases and subscriptions when you buy Kairos Premium. Apple’s privacy policy applies to those transactions.

RevenueCat helps us manage subscriptions and premium access. RevenueCat may receive purchase-related identifiers. See their policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Google AdMob serves the optional rewarded ads in Kairos. When you choose to watch an ad to unlock an extra guided prayer or Communion session, Google may collect and process limited information — such as a device advertising identifier, IP address, and general usage and interaction data — to deliver ads, measure their performance, and prevent fraud and abuse. See Google’s advertising policies and Google’s privacy policy.

Advertising

Ads in Kairos are rewarded and optional: they only appear if you actively choose to watch one to unlock an additional guided prayer or Communion session instead of subscribing to Premium. Kairos Premium removes ads entirely.

We request non-personalized ads only, meaning ads are not targeted based on your personal profile or interests. Non-personalized ads still use limited information (such as coarse location derived from IP address and contextual signals) for frequency capping, measurement, and fraud prevention. We do not use the App Tracking Transparency identifier (IDFA) for cross-app tracking.

Permissions

  • Notifications: optional, for daily reminders you configure.
  • Photo library: only if you choose to save a shared image to your gallery.

What we do not do

  • We do not use your personal data to show you personalized or interest-targeted ads (we request non-personalized ads only).
  • We do not track you across other companies’ apps and websites.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not require you to create an account.

Children

Kairos is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and we request non-personalized ads.

Data retention and deletion

Local app data is removed when you uninstall the app. Purchase records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat, and advertising data is retained by Google, according to their respective policies.

Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Contact us and we will help where we can.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the new version at this URL and update the “Last updated” date.
